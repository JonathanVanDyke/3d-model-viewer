Parixit Davé

Director of Emerging Technologies

Parixit Davé was one of the original founders of the Emerging Technologies Consortium and now leads the Emerging Technologies team within CUIT. As the team is central to CUIT, Parixit is able to connect not just with faculty but also many of the current technological leaders in the world of emerging technology.

Jonathan Van Dyke

Emerging Technologist

Jonathan Van Dyke is the technologist of the small Emerging Technologies team. He has a background in engineering, research, and has been the technical lead on projects ranging from supercomputing to aircraft systems. New to the group, Jonathan has been striving to learn as much as he can about the current Extended Reality Industry and how Columbia can best put it to use.
